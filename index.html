Welcome to the Live Weather App by Pytanic. This application is designed to provide real-time weather updates for cities around the world in a clean, simple, and responsive interface.

Home Page:

When you open the website, you are greeted by a clean home page that acts as the starting point for checking live weather conditions.

Prominently featured in the center is a search bar , where you can enter the name of any city in the world to look up its weather information. Just above the search bar, there's a cloud-shaped button that you can click to submit your search and continue to the weather results page. In the top-right corner, you'll find quick navigation links to Contact, Docs, and Donate pages — each of which is explained in detail below.

Every page within the application—whether it's the weather results, contact, documentation, or donation page—includes a Home button . This button allows users to return to the main home screen at any time.

Weather Page:

After entering a city name on the home page and clicking the cloud button, you are directed to the Weather Results Page, where detailed weather information is displayed in a neat and readable format.

At top left corner Home button is available, allowing you to return to the main page with a single click. At the top of the weather results section, the name of the city and its country is prominently displayed. Just below this, on the bottom left you’ll find the weather condition (e.g., Clear, Rainy) accompanied by a matching icon for quick visual reference. Just right to the weather condition, the current temperature is clearly shown in celsius, making it the focal point of the display. On the right side, several detailed weather metrics are displayed. These include the "feels like" temperature (in degrees Celsius), humidity (expressed as a percentage), cloud coverage (in percentage), wind speed (measured in meters per second), wind direction (in degrees), and visibility, which is shown in kilometers with a maximum value of 10 km.

All of this weather data is sourced from the reliable API service openweathermap.org.

Contact Page:

After clicking the Contact in the navigation menu at the top right corner of the main page, you’ll be directed to the Contact Page. Whether you have questions, feedback, or want to report an issue, this is where you can reach out.

The page includes three fields: Name, Email, and Message. Users can enter their contact information and describe their inquiry or feedback in the message box. Once the form is filled, clicking the Send button will submit your message. Like other pages in the app, a Home button is available to take you back to the main page at any time.

Donate Page:

When you click the Donate button in the top-right navigation of the main page, you’ll be directed to the Donate page. This page provides an option to support the project financially, purely as a gesture of appreciation.

The page includes a cryptocurrency section, where you can find wallet addresses for popular currencies such as USDT (on BEP20, Ethereum, and Tron networks), as well as XRP, Ethereum and BTC. A Fiat currency section is also included, although donations through fiat (such as bank transfer) are not available yet and will be added soon.

To the right of each wallet address, there's a button that opens a popup displaying the QR code for that specific address, making it easy to scan and send crypto directly.
